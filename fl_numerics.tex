\begin{abstract}
We present a finite element scheme for the numerical approximation of the solution to a non-local Poisson equation involving the one-dimensional fractional Laplacian on the interval $(-L,L)$. As an application, we compute the numerical approximation of the null-control for the corresponding parabolic equation.  
\end{abstract}
